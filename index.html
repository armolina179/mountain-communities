ABOUT

Mountain Communities of the World shares stories, context, and data about mountain cultures & landscapes, highlighting their importance and resilience in our rapidly changing world.

Why Mountain Communities Matter

Mountains support biodiversity, freshwater, and cultural heritage, yet face isolation, climate risk, and economic precarity.

Key Statistics

Focus area
Statistic
Population
Over 1 billion people live in mountain regions worldwide.
Biodiversity
Mountains are home to about 25% of terrestrial biodiversity.
Water Resources
Mountains provide 60–80% of the world’s freshwater resources.
Cultural Diversity
Hundreds of indigenous languages and cultures are found in mountain areas.
Climate Change Impact
Mountain regions are experiencing climate change at twice the global average rate.
